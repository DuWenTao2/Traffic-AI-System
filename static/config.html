Instructions

How to Configure Areas:
  1. Select a video source from the dropdown menu
  2. Select the area type you want to configure
  3. Click "Draw Polygon" to create an area or "Draw Line" for line-based areas
  4. Click on the canvas to add points to your polygon/line
  5. Double-click to complete your shape
  6. Click "Save Configuration" to save your changes
